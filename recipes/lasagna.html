Lasagna

The best Lasagna

This classic lasagna is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This is great for feeding a big family, and freezes well, too.

Ingredients

For the meat sauce:

  • 2 teaspoon extra virgin oil
  • 1 pound ground beef chucl
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced (about 3/4 cup)
  • 1/2 large ball pepper (green, red, or yellow), diced (about 3/4 cup)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can good-quality tomato sauce
  • 3 ounces tomato paste (half a 6-ounce can)
  • 1 (14 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons choppped fresh oregano, or 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley (preferably flat leaf), pakced
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch garlic powder and/or garlic salt
  • 1 tablespoon red or white wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon to 1/4 cup sugar (to taste, optional)
  • Salt

To assemble the lasagna

  • 1/2 pound dry lasagna noodles (requires 9 lasagna noodles - unbroken)
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 1/2 (24 ounces) mozzarella cheese, grated or sliced
  • 1 1/4 (4 ounces) freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Preparation

  1. Put pasta water on to boil
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Cook the bell pepper, onions, garlic, add back the beef
  4. Trabsfer to medium sized pot, add tomatoes and remaining sauce ingredients to build the sauce
  5. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles
  6. Heat the oven to 375Â°F
  7. Assemble the lasagna
  8. Bake
  9. Cool and serve
